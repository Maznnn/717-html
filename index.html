Alabaj

Description of the breed:

  • Breed name - Alabai or Central Asian Shepherd Dog
  • Country of origin - Regions of Central Asia
  • Time of appearance of the breed - XX century
  • Breed type - belongs to the mountain and Swiss cattle dogs, but now they are used as watchdogs
  • Weight of an adult dog - 40-65 kg (females), 50-79 kg (males)
  • Life span -12-15 years

American Akita

Description of the breed:

The dogs of this breed are very muscular and strongly built, with powerful paws, and a large head in the form of a triangle gives them a slightly "bearish" look. The coat of American Akitas varies in length in different parts of the body - the shortest on the muzzle and ears, and the longest (more than 6–7 cm) on the tail. The color of the American Akita is any, including white brindle, spotted, piebald - with or without a mask (whites do not have a mask), or with a spot on the forehead. Spotted on a white background, evenly spaced spots covering the head and more than one third of the body. The undercoat may differ in color from the coat color.

We recommend buying an American Akita puppy only in trusted kennels, be sure to “get to know” the puppy better and carefully ask the breeder about the health and c haracter of the parents of your future dog.
